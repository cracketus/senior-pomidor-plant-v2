About

Senior Pomidor is an edge telemetry project for tracking tomato plant conditions with soil, air, light, leaf temperature, power, and camera signals. The edge node is under maintenance, so the public metrics feed is paused until telemetry is restored. This page is a small public index for the project and its upcoming research outputs.
