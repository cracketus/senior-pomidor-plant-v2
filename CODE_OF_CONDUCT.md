# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Participants are expected to keep discussion respectful, focused, and constructive. Harassment, personal attacks, and disclosure of private information are not acceptable.

## Scope

This code applies to repository issues, pull requests, discussions, and any project-maintainer spaces connected to Senior Pomidor.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior to the project maintainer. Reports will be reviewed with attention to privacy and context.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior makes productive collaboration difficult or unsafe.
